Having two properties (signedIn and notSignedIn) to represent one piece of data (whether or not the user is signed in) is the sort of thing that makes most programmers itch uncontrollably.

Fortunately mustache has us covered. We can use an inverted section:

{{^signedIn}}<!-- not-signed-in block -->{{/signedIn}}

Replace the {{#notSignedIn}} section with a {{^signedIn}} section. Then, remove the references to notSignedIn in the JavaScript. Then breathe a sigh of relief – doesn't that feel better?
